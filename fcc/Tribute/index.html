NATASHA ROMANOFF

Natalia Alianova "Natasha" Romanova was one of the most talented spies and assasins of the entire world and also a member of the "Original Six" Avengers. She was recruited into The Red Room program by a Russian named Dreykov when she was only a child. She had to live undercover as the daughter of Melina Vostokoff and Alexei Shostakoff, in the United States of America. Then she was taken to the Red Room to undergo extensive physical,mental, emotional and psychological conditioning and graduated as a Widow. She worked under KGB and was then recruited to S.H.I.E.L.D. She was also one of the founding members of The Avengers and helped recruiting Tony Stark(Iron Man) and Dr. Bruce Banner(Hulk) to the Avengers Initiative.
